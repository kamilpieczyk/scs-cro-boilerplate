ScS - your sofas, flooring & furniture specialists

Over 100 years ago, in 1894, we began our journey to provide high quality sofas and flooring at excellent value for money. Over a century later and we’re still doing just that. From our humble beginnings in the city of Sunderland, Tyne and Wear, where our head office is still based today, we now sell sofas, flooring and a large range of fantastic furniture from over 100 stores up and down the country and online.

Find your dream sofa

There’s nothing better than coming home after a long day and retreating to the sanctuary of your sofa. Quite often the hub of any home, the sofa is a place where you can snuggle up with a loved one, relax with the family or enjoy a good catch up with a friend. We’re proud to stock a huge variety of sofas, from traditional 2 seaters and 3 seaters, corner sofas, recliner options, sofa beds and everything in between, to suit your style and your home.

Our superb selection of sofas come in a range of high quality finishes, including 100% genuine leather and soft touch fabrics; from contemporary to timeless looks and bold to classic colour schemes.

We’re also proud to partner with some of the most well-known brands and interior designers in the country, bringing you, our customers, fabulously designed furniture at affordable prices.

What’s more, many of our sofas are handmade in the UK, bringing you the best of British design and craftsmanship, so you can rest assured you are getting a high quality piece of furniture you’ll love for years to come.

Transform your space with our carpets and flooring

We also offer an extensive range of fantastic flooring. From carpets, vinyl, luxury vinyl and laminate options, we have an amazing array of flooring to suit all needs and budgets. From plush high pile carpets to durable and easy to clean flooring. Whether you’re looking for something luxurious and soft underfoot to something hardwearing to withstand those high-traffic areas – we’ve got your flooring needs covered.

Update your home with our faboulous furniture

From dining tables and chairs, to sideboards and coffee tables, we carry a fantastic range of home furniture to help you update your home. Whether you’re just starting out or completing a newly decorated room, we have something to suit all styles and budgets to help you add the finishing touches to your home. From beautiful wooden designs to contemporary high gloss finishes, you’ll find the perfect piece to compliment your unique style at ScS.

We also offer an impressive selection of lighting options, from timeless table lamps to contemporary floor lamps. Set the mood for a relaxing sanctuary or go for something big and bright. Whatever ambiance you’re creating, you’ll find that extra glow in our excellent range of lighting.

Take a look at our fantastic range of sofas, flooring and home furniture. Whatever stage of home life you’re in – whether you’ve just moved house or adding some finishing touches – we’re here to help you find your next perfect piece – helping create the home you love.
